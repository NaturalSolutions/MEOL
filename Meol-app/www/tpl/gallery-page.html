    Use the Discover Section to explore how organisms in each game collection are related to each other. The graph shows how scientists group species into higher ranks, such as a genus or family, based on shared traits or characteristics.
    Insects, for example, all share the trait of having 3 body parts. You can expand a section of the graph by clicking on the plus sign on a label or you can expand or collapse all the branches at once by clicking on the plus or minus button at the top of the screen.
