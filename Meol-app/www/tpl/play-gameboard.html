    M-EOL game

    M-EOL game depicts the travel of an explorer discovering different species around the world. Become this explorer by answering questions about presence of particular species on each visited continent. Depending on your answers, points are awarded and you advance in the quest. During your quest, you will be presented with the species taken from a particular collection. Earning points will unlock new collections. More than 20 000 points are needed to unlock a collection.

    How to earn points

    Throughout your expedition, you are asked multiple choice questions. Each correct answer will earn you up to 1000 points.
    The number of earned points depends on the level of difficulty of the asked question:

    If species has more than a continent, easier it is!
    1000 – (10*number of continents) [from 2 continents]

    If species is endangered, more it is known, easier it is!
    1000 – (10 * conservation status weight)

    Conservation status weighting:
    LC = 3; NT = 5; VU = 8; [CR, EW, EX] = 21

    If several answers are corrects, easier it is!
    1000 – (50*number of correct answers) [from 2 correct answers]

    Unfortunately, the difficulties may be cumulated!

    Bonus

    The key to getting a high score and to augment your chances to quickly unlock a new collection is to obtain bonuses.
    A bonus occurs when you successfully answer a series of questions in a row.
    From 3 correct answers, the bonus is calculated according to a Fibonacci sequence:

    • 3 correct answers in a row: 3 * 100
    • 4 correct answers in a row: 5 * 100
    • n correct answers in a row: Fn * 100

    The 10 first Fibonacci numbers Fn for n = 3, ... , 20 are:

    F3F4F5F6F7F8F9F10F11F12
    3581321345589144233

    If you have bad answer, you have to beat last record to obtain new bonus.
